What time is it?
it's chill o'clock

Chill O'clock is an intentionally imprecise clock. The day, which is from sunrise til sunset and varies with the seasons, is divided into 8 parts. The night is divided into 4 parts.

ℹ️ TIP: press +/- to increase/decrease the size of the clock

Chill O'clock needs to know where you are to determine when sunrise and sunset are.

Don't know your coordinates? Enter your city:

For example the wikipedia value of 33°27′N 112°04′W gives:

  • latitude: 33.27 (N is positive, S is negative)
  • longitude: -112.04 (E is positive, W is negative)
